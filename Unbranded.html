We want every qualified student to be able to afford a Haverford College education, and in turn, to contribute their distinctive gifts and perspectives to our community. That’s why we meet the full demonstrated need of all admitted students, including a no-loans policy for all families making less than $60,000 a year and a $3,000 cap on student loans for everyone.

We believe in investing in the next generation of Fords, which is why our average total grant aid package is more than $62,000.

In fact, 51% of all students receive some form of aid, and many others benefit from College-funded student employment and paid leadership positions like The Honor Council Committee, Peer Academic Facilitators and Admission Office Fellows (shameless plug).

We know that selecting a college is stressful—and we are honored that you are interested in Haverford. Providing financial aid information like this is one way we want to empower you to make the right choice for you. We see it as a great privilege to review applications and look forward to reviewing yours if you feel Haverford is the place where you will thrive.
